About iStockCheck:

iStockCheck is a free tool to provide Apple iPhone X stock notifications for Apple Stores in the UK.

iStockCheck was developed by Andrew Bennet; it is the successor to a previous incarnation which ran as a native Mac and Windows app. iStockCheck is open source, released under the MIT licence. Feel free to contribute!
